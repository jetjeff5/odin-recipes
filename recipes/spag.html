Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and Meatballs is a traditional Italian pasta dish typically consisting of speghetti noodles and marinara sauce

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add dry spaghetti to the boiling water
  3. Boil spaghetti until a noodle sticks to the wall when thrown
  4. Drain water from noodles and add to a bowl
  5. Combine marinara sauce with the noodles and stir
  6. Serve and top with parmesan cheese and basil
