Smores

Description

Smores are described as a sandwich consisting of graham crackers, toasted marshmallows, and half of a chocolate bar

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate Bar

Steps

  1. Break a Graham Cracker in half
  2. Roast a marshmallow over a fire until golden brown
  3. Stack the roasted marshmallow and half a chocolate bar between both halves of the Graham Cracker
