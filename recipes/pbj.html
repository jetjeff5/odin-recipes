Pb&j

Description

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches, also commonly known as pb&js, are a popular sandwich consisting of bread, peanut butter, and jelly

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. Get 2 pieces of bread
  2. On one piece of bread, spread peanut butter evenly
  3. On the other piece of bread, evenly spread your favorite flavor of jelly
  4. Combine the 2 to make a sandwich
